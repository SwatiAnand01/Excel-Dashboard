# Excel-Dashboard
This interactive Sales Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of sales performance across multiple dimensions, enabling quick and data-driven decision-making. The dashboard highlights key business metrics such as Total Sales (₹8,06,59,661), Average Delivery Time (5.5 days), and Average Customer Rating (3) at the top for instant performance tracking.

The dashboard includes detailed visualizations and filters to analyze sales trends effectively:

Brand-wise Sales Contribution: Displays the percentage share of sales generated by major brands such as Sony, Dell, Boat, Samsung, Nike, and others.
Geographical Analysis: A map of India showing region-wise sales distribution to identify high-performing states and locations.
Category & Customer Filters: Interactive slicers for product category, order platform, gender, region, and order date allow users to customize insights dynamically.
Product-wise Sales Breakdown: Treemap visualization representing the contribution of products like keyboards, headphones, laptops, phones, cameras, and accessories.
Platform-wise Orders: Pie charts comparing sales/orders from Mobile App vs Website channels.
Monthly Performance Trend: Horizontal bar chart showing month-wise sales percentages and seasonal performance patterns.
Payment Method Analysis: Column chart comparing transaction distribution across Card, Cash on Delivery, Net Banking, and UPI payments.
Return Analysis: Doughnut chart displaying returned vs non-returned orders to evaluate customer satisfaction and product quality.

Overall, the dashboard is designed to help businesses monitor sales performance, customer behavior, regional trends, and operational efficiency in a visually engaging and easy-to-understand format.
